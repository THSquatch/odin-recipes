Banana Bread

Description

Indulge in the delightful aroma and mouthwatering taste of homemade banana bread. This beloved classic treat is a true crowd-pleaser, combining the natural sweetness of ripe bananas with a moist and tender texture. With every bite, you'll experience the comforting flavors that make banana bread a timeless favorite. Imagine sinking your teeth into a slice of warm, freshly baked bread, as the delicate aroma of bananas fills the air. The sweetness of the bananas is perfectly balanced, creating a treat that is both comforting and satisfying. Enjoy it as a breakfast indulgence, an afternoon pick-me-up, or a delightful dessert. Whether served plain, slathered with butter, or paired with a cup of coffee, this banana bread is sure to bring a moment of pure bliss and leave you craving more. Get ready to experience the simple pleasure of homemade baking with this irresistible banana bread.

Ingredients

  • 3-4 very ripe bananas
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup butter (1 stick)
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Cream the wet ingredients (butter, sugar, bananas, and eggs) in a bowl until well incorporated.
  2. In a separate bowl sift all the dry ingredients together (flour, baking soda, and salt).
  3. Carefully mix dry ingredients into the batter in 1/3rds.
  4. Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes to one hour.
